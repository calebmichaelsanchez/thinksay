Text: THINKSAY VP/Promotion DAN PEARSON has been handed additional responsibilities, and will now be companywide VP, and add to his current responsibilities by supervising efforts in the areas of Sports Marketing and Branding. PEARSON most recently spearheaded the label's campaign for CHRIS WALLACE's debut single, "Remember When (Push Rewind)," which became one of the biggest indie singles of 2012.
