Text: ThinkSay Records is an independent music company founded by Brad Davidson and Ben Singer, two music industry executives with a track record of success, innovation and passion throughout their careers. Respectively, they have helped launched the careers of such artists as Train, John Mayer, Jessica Simpson, Beyonce and Andy Grammer amongst others. ThinkSay Records specializes in Pop, Rhythm and Pop/Rock, and their first release for Caroline will be the debut album from Chris Wallace, formerly of the White Tie Affair. Chris has already achieved great success with his 2013 debut single, “Remember When (Push Rewind)” which soared to The Top 20 on the Top 40 chart.
