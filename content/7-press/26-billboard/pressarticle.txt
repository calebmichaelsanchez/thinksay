Text: Caroline Distribution has announced the addition of ThinkSay Records to its roster.

ThinkSay is an independent music company founded in 2012 by industry executives Brad Davidson and Ben Singer, who have helped launch the careers of such artists as Train, John Mayer and Jessica Simpson.

"Caroline is thrilled to come together with ThinkSay," Caroline president Dominic Pandiscia said in a press statement. "Brad Davidson and Ben Singer have established a great partnership with both creative vision, and marketing prowess.
